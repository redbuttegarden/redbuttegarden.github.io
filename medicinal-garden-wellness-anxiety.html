ANXIETY


Feeling overwhelmed and anxious?

Plants contain a variety of chemical compounds that can affect our brains in different ways. Often we experience these effects through smell. Below are some plants that might help lesson stress through aromatherapy. When you visit the Garden, seek them out and give them a sniff.



PLANTS AND COMPOUNDS


Lavandula angustifolia

Common Name: Lavender


Melissa officinalisi

Common Name: Lemon Balm


Trachelospermum jasminoides

Common Name: Jasmine


When you visit the Medicinal Garden at Red Butte Garden, look for the grey plant indentification signs located near the plants to learn more about these plants and other cancer fighting flora.
