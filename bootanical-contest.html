PUMPKIN DECORATING CONTEST


Feeling Crafty?

While at Garden After Dark you may choose an Oz figure at the Emerald City to take home and decorate yourself using at least one natural material like an acorn cap, flowers, or a leaf. Snap a picture of your completed masterpiece and send it to us. One lucky entry in each category will win an Oz-some prize package. What will you create?

Complete the CRAFTING CONTEST ENTRY FORM online and submit a photo of your creation by November 8, 2019. Images will be shared on the Garden’s social media channels.

EYE SPY FLYING MONKEYS CONTEST


How many Flying Monkeys can you find in Oz?

Keep count of the Flying Monkeys you find hidden throughout Oz. Enter your guess online at FLYING MONKEYS ENTRY FORM to be entered to win one of six FREE Family or Circle of Friends Memberships to Red Butte Garden.
