BOOTANICAL IN THE GARDEN INSTAGRAM CONTESTS





PUMPKIN DECORATING CONTEST


Halloween is almost here! Get into the spirit of the season and join Red Butte Garden’s first Pumpkin Decorating Contest. One winner will receive an awesome prize package, including a free Garden Membership.


About the Contest

Contestants of all ages are invited to carve, paint, or sculpt a pumpkin utilizing all of their creative ideas and skills to transform it from an ordinary squash into something spectacular.


When your creation is finished, snap a selfie with your pumpkin and upload it to Instagram. Don’t forget to tag us using @redbuttegarden and #redbuttegarden (see contest rules below). Then, encourage your friends, family, and followers to vote for your pumpkin by liking your photo. At the end of the voting period, the pumpkin with the most likes on Instagram will win its decorator a great prize package.

COSTUME CONTEST


Are you ready to claim bragging rights for the best Halloween costume of 2020? Visit BOOtanical at Red Butte Garden and enter our Costume Contest. One lucky winner will receive an awesome prize package, including a free Garden Membership.


About the Contest

Visit BOOtanical at Red Butte Garden this October, stop by our Selfie Booth, snap a picture of your costume, and upload it to Instagram.


Don’t forget to tag us using @redbuttegarden and #redbuttegarden (see contest rules below). Then, encourage your friends, family, and followers to vote for your costume by liking your photo. At the end of the voting period, the costume with the most likes on Instagram will win a great prize package.

PUMPKIN CARVING CONTEST RULES & PRIZES


PUMKIN CARVING CONTEST RULES
  • Registration is free and open to pumpkin decorators of all ages and abilities
  • Contest begins October 1, 2020 and ends at midnight on October 31, 2020
  • Contestants give permission for Red Butte Garden to share their original entry photos on all social media sites
  • Enter by submitting a photo of yourself with your decorated pumpkin on Instagram tagging @redbuttegarden and using #redbuttegarden. Remember, your Instagram account MUST be public for us to find and view your photo
  • Pumpkins may be carved, painted, sculpted, or decorated by any other method. It is acceptable to add props or additional materials to the pumpkin for decoration
  • Red Butte Garden retains the right to refuse entry to any submission it deems inappropriate
  • Voting is limited to one like per entry
  • Winners will be determined by the greatest number of likes on Instagram. Prizes will be awarded for the most votes by midnight on October 31, 2020. Winners will be notified by Friday, November 6, 2020
  • Voting is limited to one like per entry
