A Red Butte Garden membership gives you the opportunity to experience every season uniquely, connect with nature in a rewarding way, and maybe attend a few concerts featuring world-renowned musicians in the beautiful Red Butte Garden Amphitheatre.

Membership benefits include Garden admission for 12 months, access to member-only events, and free admission to public events and programs such as Family Fun Nights, Orchid and Bonsai Shows, art exhibits, and guided Garden tours.

Need the perfect gift for your favorite garden enthusiast? Share the beauty of the Garden through a Gift Membership at any level. Find your perfect fit below.

Please note: Garden Memberships are not refundable or transferable, and Membership does not guarantee concert ticket availability.
For a complete list of membership policies, please refer to the Garden Policies section below:

Garden Membership Policies

  • Memberships are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Member events, programs, and discounts apply to cardholders only.
  • Cardholder must be present to receive benefits. Photo ID may be required.
  • Unlimited General Admission does not include concerts, Garden After Dark, or other separately ticketed events.
  • Only the Garden-Flex and Family-Flex membership levels allow a guest to be named as a cardholder. All other levels have set cardholder allowances.
  • You may apply same-day admission costs to the purchase of a membership. Offer is only available at the Visitor Center desk on the day that admission to the Garden was purchased.
  • Membership purchase does not guarantee Red Butte Garden Outdoor Concert Series tickets or access to other ticketed events.
  • Only Garden-Flex Membership level and above receive member-rate concert tickets and access to Garden Member Concert Series ticket presales.

Automatic Membership Renewal


Make renewals effortless with automatic membership renewal!

  • Receive two email reminders - one the month prior to expiration and one the month of expiration
  • Charges your card on your expiration date each year
  • Renews at current membership price
  • Renews at level chosen during initial auto renew transaction, or if you upgrade later, at the level you choose upon upgrading.
  • Saves garden resources and trees
  • Available online only
