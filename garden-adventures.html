Ecosystem Terrariums



Do you know what an ecosystem is? Learn about this important part of the natural world from the comfort of your home. Using an interactive workbook and fun online resources, you’ll discover what an ecosystem is and why it needs plants to function. Then use what you’ve learned to create an ecosystem terrarium of your very own.

Each kit contains everything you need to make a terrarium, including jars, plants, decorations, and more. Plus, you’ll get exclusive access to a downloadable PDF workbook to help guide your ecosystem explorations.

Sproutin’ Seeds



Spring is here, and it’s the perfect time to start thinking about your summer garden. Learn about the role seeds play in the plant life cycle and discover some of the amazing seeds found in the natural world. Then, get a head start on your garden with a DIY seed-starter kit perfectly suited to growing plants indoors.

Each kit contains everything you need to start seeds, including seeds, growing kits, and more. Plus, you’ll get exclusive access to a downloadable PDF workbook to help guide your seed investigations.
