Garden Adventures classes have been postponed until further notice.

Please check our Virtual Resources webpage for family-friendly, nature-based Boredom Buster activities that can be done at home.

Garden Adventures are classes for children ages 4-12 and a parent.

Each unique class focuses on a different topic and seeks to help kids connect with plants while encouraging them to explore the wonders of the natural world.
Classes are designed for parents or caregivers to attend and participate with their child.
Class prices vary depending on the take-home craft and include one child and a caregiver. Limit one adult/caregiver per child.
