Plants That Heal - Emotional & Mental Wellness

FOCUS & MEMORY


Sometimes people are so busy they may have trouble focusing on what they are doing and keeping track of things.

Try to memorize these plants and find them in the Medicinal Garden to learn how they can help to keep mind and memory sharp.


PLANTS MAY HELP


Ginkgo biloba

Common Name: Maidenhair tree


Centella asiaticai

Common Name: Gotu Kola


Rosmarinus officinalis

Common Name: Rosemary


When you visit the Medicinal Garden at Red Butte Garden, look for the grey plant indentification signs located near the plants to learn more about these plants and more.
