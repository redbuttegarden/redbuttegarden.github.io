We are now half way through December. Are you wondering if there is any color left here at Red Butte Garden? Well wonder no more. Take a look at the photos below, better yet, come to the Garden to see for yourself!

Nannyberry Viburnum    Viburnum lentago

You better hurry to see these vibrant fruits, they won't be able to hang on for much longer.

Orangerie Living Wall

While at the Garden, take a break from the cold and stop inside the Orangerie to see the Living Wall, you won't be sorry.

Korean Barberry    Berberis koreana

This bush is raining red berries!

European Cranberrybush Viburnum    Viburnum opulus

You can't miss these bright berries in the Medicinal Garden!

Here at Red Butte Garden we keep our pathways clear of snow and ice, so come see the Garden and behold its winter splendor.
