It is the final Friday of the month and the Garden is looking quite flowery. Come enjoy all the new blooms on this wonderful overcast day.

Cobra Lily    Arisaema ringens

While in the Herb Garden, look carefully for the flowers. They are hidden by the leaves.

Claret Cup Cactus    Echinocereus triglochidiatus

This red beauty is putting on a show near Hobb's Wayside in the Children's Garden.

Tufted Evening Primrose    Oenothera caespitosa

Fragrant and beautiful, this native can be found growing along the Great Wall and in the Natural Area.

Shepherd's Warning Geranium    Geranium sanguineum 'Shepherd's Warning'

You can't miss this stunning geranium growing across the path from the Oak Leaf Sculpture.

CheyenneÂ® Mock Orange    Philadelphus lewisii 'PWY01S'

Look for this fragrant shrub growing behind the Rose House.

Woods' Rose    Rosa Woodsii

Another beautiful native, this rose grows in various locations in the Garden as well as in the Natural Area.

Common Comfrey    Symphytum officinale

The leaves were once used for herbal teas. Find this perennial in the Herb Garden.

Don't delay your visit. These blooms won't last forever!
