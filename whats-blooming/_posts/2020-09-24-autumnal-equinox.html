Now that we have had the Autumnal Equinox, it is officially Fall. This is one of the best times to visit the Garden with the cooler temperatures.


Japanese Anemone   Anemone x hybrida 'Pamina'

This perennial blooms for a long time.


Autumn Saffron   Colchicum autumnale

This autumn bloomer is actually quite toxic!


Sunflower   Helianthus annuus

This Utah native is also native to the whole U.S.



The following are a few of the different cultivars of Red Hot Poker plants that can be found in the Water Pavilion.


Red Hot Poker   Kniphofia 'Papaya Popsicle'

Dwarf Red Hot Poker   Kniphofia 'Mango Popsicle'

Red Hot Poker   Kniphofia 'Lemon Popsicle'

Pearly Everlasting   Anaphalis margaritacea

Don't these Utah Native flowers look like eye balls?
