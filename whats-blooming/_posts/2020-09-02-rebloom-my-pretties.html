I took a walk throught the Garden today and was completely surprised by how many roses are reblooming. There are so many different roses blooming that if you missed the first flush, you should really get up here to see this one.

Crystalline™ Rose   Rosa 'ARObipy'

Creamy white double flowers adorn this beautiful shrub.


Dicky® Rose   Rosa 'DICkimono'

These salmon-colored beauties give off a lovely sweet scent.


Summer Fashion Rose   Rosa 'JACale'

As they open, the flowers are light pink and darken with age.


Starry Night Rose   Rosa 'ORAwichkay'

Small five-petaled flowers cover this shrub throughout the season.


Sally Holmes Rose   Rosa 'Sally Holmes'

Just as one flush of blooms end, another one replaces it.


Fragrant Cloud Rose   Rosa 'TANellis'

This hybrid tea rose produces flowers up to five inches across.


The temperatures have cooled slightly, so come check out this second flush of roses before the next wave of heat arrives.
