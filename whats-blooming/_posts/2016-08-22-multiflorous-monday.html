The Garden is in full bloom with no shortage of flowers or bees! Come break up your monotonous Monday by enjoying all the floral beauty the Garden has to offer!

Wild Passion Flower    Passiflora incarnata

If you look closely, you can see a bee being doused on its back with pollen while searching for nectar. Isn't that a cool trick?! Stay tuned for the sweet edible fruit to come! It can be seen in the Children's Garden.

Belladonna Delphinium    Delphinium elatum 'Bellamosum'

While in the Children's Garden, you can't miss this vibrant blue flower, talk about mesmerizing!

Rocky Mountain Raspberry    Rubus deliciosus

It's a green bee! How cool is that! This white flower will be followed by edible purple fruit.

Blue Mist Spiraea    Caryopteris x clandonensis 'Blue Mist'

More bees! If you are out looking for bees, this seems to be the plant to visit along the Floral Walk.

Mondays are dreaded by many people, if you are one of those people, or even if you are not, come brighten your day by visiting the Garden and appealing your senses.
