We're now open to the public! Don't let July pass you by without visiting the Garden.


Swamp Leather Flower   Clematis crispa

This semi-woody twining vine is native to floodplain forests, marshes, and swamps of the Southeastern United States.


Ice Breaker Korean Fir   Abies koreana 'Kahouts Ice Breaker'

This truly is an ice breaker plant! Try NOT to start a conversation while looking at this cute miniature specimen.


Inca IceTM Peruvian Lily   Alstroemeria 'Koice'

These mesmerizing beauties flower all summer long.


Black-Eyed Susan    Rudbeckia fulgida var. sullivantii 'Goldsturm'

These flowers add happiness to any garden.


Red Yucca    Hesperaloe parviflora

This common name is deceiving, although it is in the same family as Yucca, it is not a true Yucca. We still love it the same though!


Before you head out, don't forget to make a reservation!
