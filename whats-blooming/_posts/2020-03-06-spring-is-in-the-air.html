Here at the Garden, the birds are chirping and even the bees are buzzing. Spring is so close, one can smell it in the air. Today was warm enough I only needed a swaeatshirt.


Winter Aconite     Eranthus hyemalis

These might be tiny but they sure do brighten peoples faces. They can be seen near the upper pond of the Water Pavilion.


Dwarf Iris     Iris reticulata

These little cuties are popping up all over the place.


Water Pavilion

Something tells me this beautiful winter scene won't be around much longer.


Silver Cyclamen     Cyclamen coum 'Silver Form'

A sure sign spring is near when these beauties come out.


Yellow Witch Hazel     Hamamelis mollis 'Primavera'

Always a joy to see this plant blooming.


Spring Meadow Saffron     Colchicum bulbocodium

These bulbs are a new edition as this bed was recently redone. Can't wait to see what else this bed has in store.


Come enjoy this beautiful pre-spring weather up here at Red Butte Garden.
