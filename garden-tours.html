Garden Guided Tours

There are many ways to explore Red Butte Garden. We offer private, public, and tram tours and hikes tailored to specific areas of the Garden year-round. Going on a Garden Tour or Natural Area Hike with our enthusiastic and knowledgeable staff is a great way to generate new enthusiasm in your group, club, or association. Garden tours are unique and take you beyond what is initially apparent to the more subtle aspects of the Garden's splendor.

SPECIALTY TOURS

Red Butte Garden also offers group tours of the Greenhouse or tours to specifically discover the Garden's Evergreens, Bulbs & Blooms, Birds, Natural Areas, or Wildflowers. Space is limited for these specialty tours.


PRIVATE WALKING & TRAM TOURS

  • Tour reservations must be requested 10 business in advance and are subject to volunteer availability.

  • Garden Walking Tours last one and a half hours and may include up to a mile of walking. Walking Tours accommodate up to 15 guests per docent. Walking Tours are available year round, weather permitting

  • Garden Tram Tours typically last one hour and may accommodate up to seven guests per docent. Tram tours are available April Through September.

  • Natural Area Tours are also available and offer a new perspective on the vast area behind the formal gardens and some of the best views of the Salt Lake Valley. May accommodate up to 15 guests per docent.

  • Private Tours cost is $30 per docent. Advance registration is required.

  • Call 801-585-5688 for more Private Tour information.
