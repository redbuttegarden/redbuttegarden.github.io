Going on a Garden Tour or Natural Area Tour with our enthusiastic and knowledgeable staff is a great way to generate new enthusiasm in your group. Garden tours are unique and take you beyond what is initially apparent to the more subtle aspects of the Garden's splendor.

PRIVATE WALKING TOURS

STARTING SOON

Enjoy Red Butte Garden all to yourself, in the company of your own group with a private walking tour. Groups of up to five can request a tour led by one of our knowledgeable volunteer docents before or after our operating hours.

  • Available tour times are Monday through Friday at 8:00 a.m., 9:00 a.m., 5:00 p.m. & 6:00 p.m.
  • Tour reservations must be requested five business days in advance and are subject to volunteer availability.
  • Garden Walking Tours last one and a half hours and may include up to a mile of walking. Walking Tours accommodate up to five guests per docent.
  • Natural Area Tours are also available and offer a new perspective on the vast area behind the formal gardens and some of the best views of the Salt Lake Valley. May accommodate up to five guests per docent.
  • Garden member Tour Price $150 per group
  • General Public Tour Price $175 per group



Please email franny.johnson@redbutte.utah.edu for more Private Tour information.

The Garden will not offer complimentary or private tram tours during the 2020 season.
