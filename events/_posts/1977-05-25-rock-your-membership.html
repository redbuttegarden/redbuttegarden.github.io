Rock Your Membership to Red Butte Garden!

Upgrade your concert experience by making an additional donation when you purchase or renew a Garden membership between now and December 31, 2020. All suggested donations of $25 will receive one entry to win an exclusive Headliner for Two Concert Donor Club Ticket Package for the next season of the Red Butte Garden Outdoor Concert Series.


Opportunity Drawing Rules:

  • All suggested donations made for contest entry must be received after November 10, 2020 and before December 31, 2020.
  • One entry for each suggested donation of $25 made with a membership purchase. Multiple suggested donations may be made with one membership purchase for additinal entries. Limit of 4 entries per person.
Ticket Package will be honored during the next Red Butte Garden Outdoor Concert Series. This package includes:
  • Two unreserved tickets to 22 pre-determined concerts* in the Full Season Package; Season Package is determined by the Garden, no substitutions.
  • Open seating on Eccles Terrace; chairs & cabaret tables provided on first come first served basis.
  • Preferred parking in Concert Club Lot; your ticket is your pass.
  • Donor express gate entrance.
  • *Number of concerts subject to change.

  • Winner will be picked at random after December 31, 2020 deadline and announced in January, 2021.
  • Red Butte Garden Staff members are ineligible for this contest.
  • In this case, suggested donations are not considered a charitable gift.
