Rock Your Membership to Red Butte Garden!


Upgrade your concert experience by making an additional donation when you renew your Garden membership between now and December 31, 2020. All donations of $25 will receive one entry to win an exclusive Headliner for Two Concert Donor Club Ticket Package for the next season of the Red Butte Garden Outdoor Concert Series.


Opportunity Drawing Rules:

  • All renewals and additional donations for entry must be received by December 31, 2020.
  • One entry for each suggested donation of $25 made with a membership purchase. Limit of 4 entries per person.
  • Winner will be announced by January 31, 2021.

Headliner for Two Concert Donor Club Ticket Package will be honored during the next Red Butte Garden Outdoor Concert Series. This package includes:

  • Two unreserved tickets to 22 pre-determined concerts* in the Full Season Package; Season Package is determined by the Garden, no substitutions
  • Open seating on Eccles Terrace; chairs & cabaret tables provided on first come first served basis.
  • Preferred parking in Concert Club Lot; your ticket is your pass.
  • Donor express gate entrance. *Number of tickets subject to change.
  • Winner will be picked at random after December 31, 2020 deadline and announced in January, 2021.
  • Red Butte Garden Staff members are ineligible for this contest.
  • In this case, suggested donations are not considered a charitable gift.

Alternate entry method: Email membership@redbutte.utah.edu

For entry to be deemed valid email must include contestant first and last name, phone number, and email address.
