2019 MONDAY FAMILY NIGHTS

JUNE 10

Divya School of Dance

Experience the art of traditional Indian dancing with the Divya School of Dance. This South Jordan-based school is dedicated to sharing Indian dancing with Utah. Enjoy the beauty and grace of classical Indian dance styles like the Bharatnatyam and the Mohiniyattam and the high-energy fun of Bollywood-style dances, all featuring a variety of youth and adult performers.

JULY 8

School of Rock

Get ready to dance to the music of the School of Rock. Based in Sandy, the School of Rock consists of two performing groups, the House Band and the Little Rock Band. Each group features musicians under the age of 18 who perform a lively mix of covers from the 70s, 80s, and 90s. Don't miss out on this performance sure to get your toes tapping and your hands clapping.

AUGUST 12

Island Traditions

Witness the culture, beauty, and magic of Polynesia with Island Traditions. This South Jordan-based dance group aims to share time-honored traditions from the islands of Hawaii, New Zealand, Samoa, and Tahiti through authentic dances, music, and costumes. Experience your own taste of the islands via interactive instruction on the Hula, the Haka, and the fast-paced Otea dance.
