REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Online class registration includes one free admission pass to Red Butte Garden.


Create a wildlife oasis in your own yard! As human activity eliminates crucial habitat, historic decreases in populations have been recorded across many wonderful and critical species. Join Red Butte Garden's botanist and curator of plant records, Jason Baker, in discovering plants suited for our area that can enhance your yard and create a haven for local wildlife.
