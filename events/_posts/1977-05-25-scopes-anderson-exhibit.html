A photographic tribute to the wonder of nature by Leslie Scopes Anderson and Mike Anderson.


Two award-winning photographers present an exhibit showcasing the beauty of birds, in the garden and other habitats around the world and throughout the seasons. Leslie and Mike have been photographing birds for more than a decade and are pleased to share their passion with you.
