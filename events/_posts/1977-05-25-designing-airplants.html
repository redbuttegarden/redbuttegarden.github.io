What plant requires no soil in which to grow, and doesn’t require a routine watering schedule?

Why its a Tillandsia, of course! Sign up for this new class on how to Grow Air Plants (Tillandsia), a New Indoor Trend! Just as easy if not easier than succulents, Tillandsia’s just may be that forget about it, no brainer plant your looking for!! In this class we will learn about these epiphytic wonders and create an exotic arrangement for home or office that is sure to last for a very long time! Students will pick up class materials prior to class at Red Butte Garden.
