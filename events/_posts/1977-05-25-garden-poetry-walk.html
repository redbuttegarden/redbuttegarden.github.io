Did you know April is National Poetry Month? From April 1 - May 31, the Garden celebrates this unique literary art by posting the eleven winning poems of our annual poety contest in stand-up displays throughout the Garden.

This year, we are featuring the poems on this page, and will post these poems in the Garden when we are able to open to the public.

Click HERE to read the winning poems.


2020 Red Butte Garden Spring Poetry Contest Winners


1. The Magnolia by Phillip Brown
2. The Spring Season by Alexa V., Youth Winner
3. Springtide Celebration by Lindsey Barr
4. City Spring by Victoria Childress
5. Spring's Herald by Jolene Whitney
6. The Storm by Olivia D., Youth Winner
7. I'm Arrested by Maurine Haltiner
8. Chrysalis by Steven Leitch
9. Uncluttering by Lin Ostler
10. The Edge of Spring by Alicia Platt
11. Spring by Ella P., Youth Winner
