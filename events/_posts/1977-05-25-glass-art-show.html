The Glass Art Guild of Utah returns to Red Butte Garden for the 15th Annual stunning show of kiln work and blown glass. Large and small pieces including garden art, decorative boxes, plates, trays, bowls, sculptural works, and jewelry pieces will display the brilliant colors of this delightful medium. Items displayed will be available for sale.

Free Garden Admission during the Holiday Open House, Saturday & Sunday, December 2 & 3 thanks to ZAP.

For more, please visit: www.glassartguild.org
