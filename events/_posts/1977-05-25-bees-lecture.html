How many bee species are in North America? 100? 200? How about 4000!

Red Butte Garden is home to about 132 different species of bees, from 34 different genre, and all six North American bee families!

Learn about the huge variety of native bees with Dr. Joseph Wilson, assistant professor of Entomology at Utah State University Tooele, and author of The Bees In Your Backyard: A Guide to North American Bees. (Princeton University Press): http://press.princeton.edu/titles/10593.html
