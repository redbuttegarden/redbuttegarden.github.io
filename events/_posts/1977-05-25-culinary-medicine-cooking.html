This series of courses aims to empower individuals with the tools and confidence to redefine healthful cooking while pursuing deliciousness! Classes will be held online via Zoom.

Students will receive recipes with a list of ingredients before classes in order to follow along with instructor. Zoom link will be sent to students one day before class.



Class Offerings

REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Online class registration includes one free admission pass to Red Butte Garden.

PRESERVING & FERMENTING A FALL HARVEST

TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 8

6-7:30PM

Join Chef and Registered Dietitian, Theresa Dvorak, to learn easy ways to preserve and ferment your favorite fall harvest so that you can enjoy deliciousness all winter long! Participants will practice preserving and fermenting a variety of foods (tomatoes, zucchini, onions, cabbage, peppers, kale, and apples) as well as prepare a meal full of local seasonal fare in this hands on cooking class. No special equipment necessary!

EATING FOR A HEALTHY IMMUNE SYSTEM

TUESDAY, OCTOBER 13

6:00-7:30PM

Join Culinary Medicine Chefs to prepare a quick, affordable, delicious meal to support your immune system. Participants will cook alongside health professionals to learn about the health benefits of a variety of easily found foods that will help to keep you healthy throughout the cold and flu season!


Can Holiday Favorites be Healthy & Delicious?

TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 10

6-7:30PM

Join Culinary Medicine Chefs in preparing holiday favorite foods that can have less sugar and fat but still full of flavor and deliciousness. Participants will learn about how to make healthful substitutions to holiday foods without losing out on flavor or satisfaction!
