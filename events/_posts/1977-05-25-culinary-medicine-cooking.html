Superfoods to help you eat happy, feel healthy

TUESDAY, JANUARY 12

6-7:30PM

This class will explore what has been found in many studies that increased fruit and vegetable consumption is associated with increased well-being, happiness, motivation, and life satisfaction. Consuming a diet high in fruit and vegetable intake is associated with a lower risk of type 2 diabetes, cancers, and respiratory conditions however, enhanced well-being is important as it creates an immediate effect after the consumption of these foods which can be more motivating leading to a more permanent and consistent intake and improve not only physical but also emotional health.

Recipes include: Roasted red pepper and chicken chowder, Healthy Corn Bread, Mini Cheesecake Bites

Cooking heart healthy for all those people in your heart

TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 9

6-7:30PM

In this class we will be preparing and discussing a Valentine’s themed meal that is sure to keep you and your loved one’s hearts healthy. We encourage attending this class with someone you care about for added fun and enjoyment! We will be discussing and preparing heart healthy foods and treats, discussing the role of fats and added sugars on heart health, as well as other important lifestyle choices for keeping your ticker ticking!

Recipes include: Heart Salad, Salmon Veggie Saute, Chocolate chia pudding
