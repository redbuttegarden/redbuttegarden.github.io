Due to the Garden closure, we are working on a Virtual Arbor Day Celebration to share with you.

Friday, April 24 - All Day


Check back on April 24 to participate in our Virtual Arbor Day Celebration activites!



As part of Utah’s official arboretum, there’s no place better than Red Butte Garden to celebrate Arbor Day!
Join us for a day filled with "tree-rific" fun and discover the vital role trees play not only in our lives, but also in the natural world.

Thanks to funding in part by Salt Lake County Zoo, Arts, and Parks Program (ZAP) Arbor Day is a ZAP Free Day!
Enjoy free Garden admission all day.
