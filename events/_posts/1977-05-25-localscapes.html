Class Times

REGISTRATION REQUIRED

Flip Your Strip

TUESDAY, JUNE 23

7-8PM

Members $5 Public $7

This class covers everything you need to know to switch your park strip from lawn to waterwise plantings. Learn about the best park strip plants to use, irrigation essentials (like how to convert an existing sprinkler system to drip), and maintenance techniques that will keep your park strip looking beautiful over time.

Introduction to Localscapes

THURSDAY, JULY 16

6:30-8:30PM

Members $5 Public $7

You've heard about Localscapes, but you don't know if this approach is right for you? Start here! If you are ready to increase the usability and curb appeal of your yard while decreasing your yard work - this class will expose you to the best way to landscape in Utah.


Localscapes University

SATURDAY, JULY 18

2-5PM

Members $16 Public $20

Ready to apply the design framework you learned in Introduction to Localscapes? This course will teach you how to apply the method correctly to ensure you achieve all the benefits a Localscape provides. We'll cover the five section steps in detail including landscape layout, irrigation basics, planting, and maintenance.

Localscapes Design

SATURDAY, AUGUST 1

1-4PM

Members $50 Public $54

Creating a good landscape plan is one of the most important steps to achieving a beautiful and functional landscape. If you’ve taken Localscapes University but want extra help laying out your design, this workshop will get your ideas onto paper. We’ll walk you through a guided design process that covers the elements of a Localscape and the basics of planting bed design without getting into the details like selecting individual plants. Each attendee should leave with a basic Localscapes design drawn to-scale.

    Requirements:
  • Attendees are required to have attended a Localscapes University.
  • Attendees must come prepared with a base map of their property drawn to scale.
