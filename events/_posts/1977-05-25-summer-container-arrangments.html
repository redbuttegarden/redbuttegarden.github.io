Class Times

SATURDAY, JULY 18

10:00AM - 11:30PM


REGISTRATION REQUIRED



Make a bold statement on your patio or porch this summer by planting a colorful assortment of annuals, perennials, and tropicals into your decorative pots and planters. Learn how to choose vibrant color combinations all while gaining a general knowledge of plant needs and care. You will learn which plants require full sun, part sun, part shade or full shade and the difference between annuals and perennials. Give your houseplants a summer vacation and take them outdoors and incorporate them into your summer container designs. Class will be taught online via Zoom and a materials kit will be available for pick up before class in order for students to follow along with designer and horticulturist, Scott Mower. Cost of materials is included in registration. Students will pick up materials kit at Red Butte Garden a few days before class.
