REGISTRATION REQUIRED


This class is designed to give you a basic understanding of the art of Bonsai - and equip you with the skills to necessary to grow and shape your own tree. Topics covered will include seasonal care of Bonsai trees, tools and supply basics, repotting with a step-by-step demo, and wiring and styling demonstrations.

There will also be a short question and answer session at the end of class. During this time, you are welcome to bring and ask specific questions about your trees.
