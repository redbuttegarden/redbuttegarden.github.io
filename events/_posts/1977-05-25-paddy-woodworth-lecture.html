Red Butte Garden Lecture Series Presents:
A Review of Global Restoration Efforts w/ Paddy Woodworth


THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 20 @ 4 - 5PM

No registration necessary.

Free for Garden members and University of Utah faculty, staff, and students (with valid ID).

Included with regular Garden admission to the public.



Paddy Woodworth, renown author, journalist, lecturer, and tour guide will discuss the “place of restoration as a global conservation strategy appropriate to the climate and biodiversity crises.”

Woodworth is a research associate at Missouri Botanical Garden and an adjunct senior lecturer at University College Dublin. He is a founder member of the Irish forum on Natural Capital and is committed to working for better communication of environmental issues nationally and to local communities and interest groups.
