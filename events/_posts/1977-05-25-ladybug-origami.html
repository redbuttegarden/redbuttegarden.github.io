Did you know ladybugs are often called a “gardener’s best friend?” They earned this title because ladybugs eat insects that damage plants like aphids, mites, and white flies. One ladybug can eat over 5,000 insects in its lifetime. Grab a square of paper and download the instructions to create one or many unique ladybugs you can use to decorate your home and remember this important insect.

Share your designs with us at #RedButteGarden.

Origami is the art of paper folding and is thought to help develop better eye-hand coordination and concentration skills.
