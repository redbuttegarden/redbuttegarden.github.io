Registration covers all 3 July classes. Students will be sent a Zoom link prior the first class.


Take a moment for yourself and join Maurena in the Garden for a moment of Mindfulness during your lunch break.

Maurena tends to focus on mindfulness meditation, but often adds a little bit of self-compassion and loving-kindness to the practice. Mindfulness Meditation can be practiced while sitting, standing, lying down, or walking.

“Mindfulness is awareness that arises through paying attention, on purpose, in the present moment, non-judgmentally.” Jon Kabat-Zinn, the founder of Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction
