Class Times

THURSDAYS, FEBRUARY 4 & 11, 6:00 - 7:30PM



An exploration of the fundamentals of soil science and its many implications and applications in gardening, farming, and ecology among other fascinating aspects. Develop a holistic viewpoint to understand the complex web of relationships between plants, geology and soil processes, nutrients, fungi, soil life, animals, water and weather patterns and more. Empower yourself to relate to plants, gardens and landscapes in a deeper, more dynamic way. Lastly, Enhance your appreciation for the complex yet simple beauty of the natural processes going on all around us.
