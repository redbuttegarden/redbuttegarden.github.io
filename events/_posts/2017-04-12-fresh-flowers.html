REGISTRATION REQUIRED


Learn the fundamentals of floral design and create traditional-style arrangements in this hands-on workshop. We'll cover the technical tricks of flower arranging, as well as composition, color, design, and discuss incorporating flowers from your own garden into arrangements. Each week, you'll have the opportunity to design and take your creations home to enjoy. Please bring a floral knife or snips, ribbon scissors, wire cutters, an apron, and gloves to each class.
