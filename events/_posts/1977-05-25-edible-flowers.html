Class Times

THURSDAY, JUNE 18 @ 6-9PM


REGISTRATION REQUIRED



Since the time of Julius Caesar and the reign of Queen Victoria, flowers have been used to enhance culinary creations and cocktails. Learn about edible flowers and which ones have the most culinary interest, how to harvest, when and how to use them. Discover some unusual or lesser known fleurs and learn an arsenal of recipes and knowledge to start up your gardening season with ideas to continue using edible flowers throughout the season. You will learn to identify plants and get tips for growing your own edible flowers. Co-sponsored with Lifelong Learning.
