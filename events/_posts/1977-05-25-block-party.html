Thanks for making the Block Party a success! Check back in spring 2020 for information about the next event.

Join us for an unforgettable evening filled with games, nature-inspired crafts, Garden-wide scavenger hunts, and more.

Bring a picnic dinner to enjoy as you listen to live local music and soak up the beauty of summer in the Garden. Then, sit back and relax as you watch a late-night screening of 2020 film to be announced on the Amphitheatre lawn, complete with a free tasty treat or two.

Don’t miss out on this fun-filled night in the Garden. Perfect for families, friends, date-night, and more.


WHAT TO BRING

Be sure to bring low-back chairs and blankets. Hats or umbrellas to block the sun are always a good idea. You are welcome to bring a cooler and pack your own picnic and beverages of choice.

Even on the hottest summer days, the Amphitheatre is located at the mouth of Red Butte Canyon and it can get chilly after sundown, so bring a jacket and other warm layers to stay cozy during the movie.

No cooking or barbeques allowed. Please pack out whatever you bring into the venue.
