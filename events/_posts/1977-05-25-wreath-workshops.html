Wreath workshops will be held online via Zoom in order to keep students, guests, staff, and volunteers safe during this unique time.

Students will be able to pick up materials prior to class in order to follow along with instructor on Zoom. Material kits include Noble Fir as the main greenery and one of the following additions: Princess Pine, Juniper, Port Orford Cedar, and Incense Cedar.


Start off your holiday season by creating a fresh wreath for your front door at our annual wreath-making workshop.
