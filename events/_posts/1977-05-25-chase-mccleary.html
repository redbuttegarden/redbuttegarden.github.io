Meet the Artist Reception
Saturday, August 18 from 1-4PM

The rugged beauty of Utah has always been enchanting to me as a lifelong resident and artist. Every time I venture into the desert or explore mountain trails, I’m inspired by the amazing wildlife and landscape that surround me. Nature is my muse and I humbly try to capture its beauty on canvas.

“It’s not what you look at that matters... it’s what you see.” - Henry David Thoreau

This show is dedicated to my lifelong friend Leigh Ann Givens.
