Looking to reduce your water consumption while keeping your garden attractive? Do you want to improve the ecological quality of your landscape? Interested in low-water plants, but not sure where to start? If so, please join us for a primer on waterwise landscaping. We will cover the basics of waterwise landscaping, including site selection, soil preparation, passive water harvesting techniques, irrigation systems, hydro-zoning, low-water lawn alternatives, design styles and plant selection. Co-sponsored with Lifelong Learning. Members pay $53; register for section 002.
