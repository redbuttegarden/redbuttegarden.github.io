Class Times

MONDAY, JULY 20 @ 6-7:30PM


REGISTRATION REQUIRED


Properly identifying plants can help avoid costly mistakes such as removing the wrong plant or applying the wrong herbicide. In this class we will learn to identify the most common weeds found in the home garden as well as best practices for control and removal. Spend an evening with Red Butte Garden Curator, Jason Baker learning how to deal with common weeds in your garden.
