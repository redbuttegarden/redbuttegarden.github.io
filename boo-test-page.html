QUESTIONS?

Pre-purchased Tickets
To help facilitate physical distancing, all BOOtanical admissions (including admission for Garden members) will be controlled through 30-minute timed intervals. Tickets are required for all guests over the age of 2. Guests aged 2 and under are free. Please reserve your tickets online.


Before arriving at BOOtanical
BOOtanical takes place primarily outside, so dress for the weather. Garden paths will be lit at night, but you may want to bring a flashlight to help guide your way. Please also bring hand sanitizer, face coverings, and snacks. Please note, all guests will be asked to carry out any trash they create (e.g. food wrappers/containers, used napkins, used utensils, etc.).


Arrival at BOOtanical
Guests should arrive at BOOtanical during their designated time slot. Garden staff will facilitate contactless entry through the Visitor Center main entrance. Please be mindful of posted guidelines throughout the Garden and follow instructions given by Garden staff, volunteers, and security.


During BOOtanical
Please practice physical distancing in the Garden and refrain from gathering in groups. Limited restrooms are available. Please be mindful of posted guidelines throughout the Garden and follow instructions given by Garden staff, volunteers, and security.


Face Coverings
Face Coverings (e.g. masks, scarves, neck gaiters, bandanas, etc.) are required on the University of Utah campus, including Red Butte Garden. Face coverings must cover both the nose and mouth and should be used wherever physical distancing measures are difficult to maintain.


Closure Areas
Certain parts of the Garden may be closed during your visit to ensure the safety of our guests, volunteers, and staff.


Costumes
Costumes are encouraged, but please make sure they conform to our Costume Policy.


BOOtanical Lights
Decorative lights turn on each night from 6 – 9 PM. Please stay on the recommended path and follow instructions given by Garden staff, volunteers, and security.


BOOtanical Activities
BOOtanical includes several onsite and virtual activities. See what’s happening in the (Insert Header Name Here) drop-down menu on this page.


Refund Policy
All ticket sales are final. No refunds, replacements, or exchanges.


Parking?
Please park in the Red Butte Garden parking lot directly in front of the main Visitor Center. Guests may drop off passengers at the front doors before parking. Additional parking is available on Colorow (the street to the south of the Red Butte Garden entrance drive).

Welcome to BOOtanical!

The seven monarchs of Halloween—the Pumpkin Queen, Vampire King, Spider Queen, Mummy King, Ghost Queen, Werewolf King, and Witch Queen—have chosen the Garden to host their annual month-long BOOtanical celebration this October. Each has a task for you to complete while you explore their temporary homes in the Garden.

Discover the ties between Halloween and the natural world. Enjoy safe activites, live local entertainment, and more. Join us for BOOtanical, an outdoor, family-friendly experience in the Garden to celebrate Halloween.
