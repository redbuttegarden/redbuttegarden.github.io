2020 Scholarship Applications open January 28 at 9 AM.

Due to a generous contribution from The Dee Foundation and Jim and Cynthia Bach, we have a limited number of scholarships available for our 2020 Summer Camps. All applications are due by 11:59 PM on February 17, 2020.

Scholarships include: *

  • Full tuition for a morning, afternoon, or all-day camp (check Summer Camp pages for availability)

To be eligible, a camper must meet one of the following qualifications:

  • Receive Free or Reduced Price Lunch in the National School Lunch Program
  • Meet the Federal Income Eligibility Guidelines for the National School Lunch Program


Please note that because funds are limited and demand for camp is high, qualifying for a scholarship does not guarantee a scholarship award or guarantee a spot in the summer camp of your choice.

Parents or guardians are responsible to provide transportation for their child to and from camp each day.
