Registration now open!

Bring your preschooler (ages 3-4) to Lil' Buds, a pint-sized class made just for them!

Each Lil' Buds class features a unique theme with fun crafts, activities, games, snacks, and hands-on fun in the Garden. Lil' Buds classes are offered once a week and are designed for caregivers to attend and participate with their Lil' Bud. Together, we'll help your Lil' Bud make new friends, prepare for preschool, develop curiosity, and explore the natural world. Classes may be taken in the full series of five or individually.

Tuition includes supplies and Garden admission for one child (ages 3-4) and a parent or caregiver.

Thursdays from 10 - 11:30 AM
Garden members: $16 per class / General public: $20 per class
