Fall 2020 Field Class Changes:

As the world continues to navigate the COVID-19 Pandemic, Red Butte Garden will not be offering in person Guided or Self-Guided visits this fall to help keep our staff, volunteers, students, and educators safe. Please note, these changes are for Fall 2020 only. The Garden will reassess school programs this November 2020 for Spring 2021 using the latest Federal, State, City, and University guidelines.




Bring a Garden Field Trip into you classroom this fall with our new virtual investigations for K-5th grade students.

Our staff will be offering free Field Class programming with SEEd aligned lessons, garden video tours, and an opportunity for a scheduled “live” visit from a Garden Educator. Conduct the program in your classroom or adapt it for at home learning.

To learn about details for this fall programming option called: “Virtual SEEd Exploration”, and to request the materials, Please click here.

Requests are processed beginning Sept 1st. Access to the garden tour videos begin after September 24th.
