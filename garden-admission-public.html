TIMED ENTRY TICKETS & RESERVATIONS

Pre-purchased tickets and entry time reservation required.

  • To help facilitate physical distancing, all Garden admissions—including admission for Garden members—will be controlled through 30-minute timed intervals that must be reserved online.
  • The first half-hour entry time of each day is reserved for seniors and high-risk individuals.
  • Tram service is currently unavailable. Guests should be prepared to walk the Garden paths with minimal assistance.
  • Please read our new COVID-19 Policies and FAQs before purchasing your tickets.
  • Cancellation Policy: If you need to cancel your reservation, please do so at least 24 hours prior to your time slot via email to: information@redbutte.utah.edu
  • Private Walking Tours are available in November. Visit our Garden Tours page to register.
  • If you are a current University of Utah student or have a discount pass, please call (801)585-0556 between 9:30am-3:30pm to make a reservation.
