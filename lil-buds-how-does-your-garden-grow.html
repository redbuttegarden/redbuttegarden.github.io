LIL' BUDS CLASSES

How Does Your Garden Grow?

For preschoolers ages 3-4 and a caregiver.


Dig into the basics of botany and learn about world of plants. We'll explore flowers, trees, shrubs, and more with activities perfectly suited for your Lil' Bud like plant-inspired art projects, fun games, plant-based snacks, botanical picture books, and more.
