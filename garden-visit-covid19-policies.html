WELCOME BACK!

RED BUTTE GARDEN COVID-19 VISITATION POLICIES



The health and safety of our guests, staff, volunteers—and our plant collections—are our top priority. Keeping in line with the latest COVID-19 guidelines issued by the Centers for Disease Control (CDC), the University of Utah, and Salt Lake City, we request that all visitors and guests adhere to the following temporary protocols if you plan to visit. These measures are necessary to ensure a safe and positive experience for everyone. We appreciate your support and shared commitment to the well-being of our community.
And most importantly, WELCOME BACK!

Please check our Virtual Resources webpage for online classes, gardening tips, and family-friendly, nature-based activities that can be done from home.

For more information regarding Red Butte Garden programming and event changes, please Click Here.

Look for the latest news about COVID-19 status on the University of Utah Dept. of Public Safety webpage.

Driving Directions


  • Guests will enter through the Visitor Center via a contactless system, checking in at the front desk before entering the Garden.
  • Guests will exit the Garden either through the turnstyle north of the Visitor Center parking lot, or via the boardwalk that leads to the north door of the Visitor Center and exit through the Visitor Center connector service doors to the parking lot.
  • Please be aware of people walking through the parking lot and of the new entrance line that will be formed outside of the Visitor Center.

  • Practice social distancing by staying six feet apart from others, including Garden workers.
  • Practice daily preventative measures such as proper hand washing, and avoid touching Garden features like sculptures, pots, fountains, etc.
  • Stay home if you do not feel well or have symptoms of COVID-19.
  • All guests will be required to wear face coverings. All staff and volunteers will be wearing face coverings while working.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

  • Pre-purchased tickets are required. To help facilitate social distancing, all Garden admissions (including admission for Garden members) will be controlled through 30-minute timed intervals that must be reserved online.
  • Before arrival: Guests should be prepared to bring their own hand sanitizer, face coverings, bottled water, and snacks, and be prepared to carry out their trash.
  • Arrival at the Garden: Guests should arrive at the Garden during their designated time slot. Garden staff will facilitate contactless entry through the Visitor Center main entrance. Guests should be mindful of instructional signs and maintain proper distancing when checking in at the front desk.
  • In the Garden: Guests will follow one-way routing throughout the Garden. When visiting, guests should practice social distancing and not gather in groups. Limited restrooms are available. Please be mindful of posted guidelines throughout the Garden, and please follow any instructions given by Garden staff members or volunteers.
  • Closure areas: Certain areas of the Garden remain temporarily closed, including the Gift Shop, portions of the Visitor Center, the Orangerie, the Children’s Garden, a small section of the Floral Walk, and all interactive fountains.
  • Tram service is currently unavailable. Guests should be prepared to walk the Garden paths with minimal assistance.
  • Questions? Call 801-585-0556 or email information@redbutte.utah.edu. We are happy to help.
  • Garden Members Only: Admission Reservations
  • General Public: Tickets and Reservations
  • Cancellation Policy: If you need to cancel your reservation, please do so at least 24 hours prior to your time slot via email to: information@redbutte.utah.edu
