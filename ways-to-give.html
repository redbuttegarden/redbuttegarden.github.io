The coming of summer 2020 has been bittersweet for all of us, with the cancellation of beloved Garden programs—concerts, summer camps, workshops and family events. Programs that are not only responsible for the creation of opportunities to learn, connect, and grow, but also for the majority of Garden revenue.
By donating today, you will bolster Red Butte Garden's ability to overcome the challenges presented by 2020.

Support the Garden and help provide quality educational programs, unique exhibitions, and help fund future research projects by our Conservation and Horticulture teams. Give back to the Garden and help us create a community that is enriched by living landscapes and connected to the natural world.

Set up a Recurring Gift

Recurring gifts are an easy and efficient way to support your favorite Garden program or initiative. For as little as $5, you can provide a steady source of support for Red Butte Garden’s award-winning educational programs, springtime bulb display, or conservation research efforts. For more information on recurring gifts, click here here or call 801-581-3341.


Double the impact of your gift - see if your employer will match your donation!

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match any charitable contributions or volunteer hours made by their employees. Visit the University of Utah Office of Development’s Matching Gift website to see if your company offers a matching gift program or contact your employers’ Human Resources Department.
