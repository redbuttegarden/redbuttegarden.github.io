LIL' BUDS CLASSES

Homes & Habitats

Day camps for preschool children (ages 3-4) and a caregiver.


Come join us, along with your lil’ bud and we’ll learn all about the animals that call Red Butte Garden home. Where do they live? What do they eat? How do they run? Why do they like living in the Garden? Learn all about these amazing creatures through art, song, numbers, patterns, sensory play, garden explorations, and more.
