Junior Naturalists (1)

Partner Camp with NHMU
June 12-16
9AM-4:30PM
SESSION 1: JUNE 12 - 16
SESSION 2: JULY 31 - AUGUST 4

Partner Camp with Natural History Museum of Utah (NHMU)

Explore the natural world with Red Butte Garden and the Natural History Museum of Utah! Spend mornings at Red Butte Garden looking at the natural world from the perspective of ants, raccoons, rocks, and more to discover the unique perspective each viewpoint gives you. In the afternoon travel to NHMU where you’ll investigate the natural processes surrounding you, including the changing seasons, the rock cycle, the water cycle, and more. Registration through NHMU.

Drop off at Red Butte Garden by 9AM and pick up at NHMU by 4:30PM.

Caregivers: Please note the curriculum for Session 1 & 2 is the same.
