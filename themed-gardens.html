THEMED GARDENS

Red Butte Garden is Utah's Botanical Garden. Covering over 100 acres, it is comprised of many different gardens suited for different kinds of plants, landscaping, and watering needs.

Themed gardens include the Four Seasons Garden, Herb Garden, Children's Garden, Fragrance Garden, Floral Walk, Medicinal Garden, Water Pavilion Garden, Rose Garden, Wildflower Meadow, Water Conservation Garden, and others. Stay tuned for more information about our themed gardens as we continue to update this page.
