Wildlife in the Garden

We are located in the foothills of the Wasatch Mountain Range which is a natural habitat for many wild animals such as deer, coyotes, bobcats, squirrels, rabbits, raccoons, snakes, and birds. For your safety and theirs, do not approach wildlife in the Garden, please respect them from a distance.
